# Rust fundamentals quiz

*If you can answer these without flipping back, you've internalised more Rust than you think.*

Twenty multiple-choice questions covering the ground we've walked together: ownership and borrowing, `Option` and `Result`, lifetimes, traits, enums, modules.
Pick an answer to lock it in and reveal the explanations, not just for the right answer, but for every distractor too, because the wrong answers are usually where the learning lives.

> [!TIP]
> No grade, no time limit, no record kept.
> Skim the explanations even for the ones you got right.
> Sometimes the *why* is more interesting than the *what*.
